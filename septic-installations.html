Why It Matters

A $10,000 Problem Hides Underground

A septic system is the most expensive component of a home that buyers never look at. A failing drain field or damaged tank can cost five figures to replace — and a standard home inspection barely touches the septic system. Before you close on a home with septic in North Georgia, have someone who actually installs and repairs these systems put eyes on it.

We evaluate the tank, the lines, and the drain field, and tell you plainly what condition the system is in, how much life it likely has left, and what it would cost to fix anything we find. That's negotiating power at the closing table — or peace of mind.

Building on raw land? The perc test comes first — it determines whether the soil can support a septic system and what type you'll need. We coordinate perc testing and county health department requirements as part of every new installation, so you know what your land can support before you commit to a build.
